Dubai
I had the best time ever in Dubai’s deserts! I rode an ATV and zoomed all over the sandy dunes.

Then, I went sand dashing in a crazy car that looked like it could flip over any minute—like a
wild roller coaster on sand!

And guess what? I even tried sand boarding, which was like sliding down a giant, sandy slide.
It was so much fun!
